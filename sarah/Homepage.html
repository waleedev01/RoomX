XRoom Business App

XRoom is a free to download app that was built with the small business owner in mind. Create a catalog to showcase your products and services. Connect with your customers easily by using tools to automate, sort and quickly respond to messages.

END-TO-END ENCRYPTION

Since most of your personal moments are shared on XRoom, we built a end-to-end encryption into the latest versions of our app. When end-to-end encrypted, only you and the person you're communicating with can read or listen to them, and nobody in between.
